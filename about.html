About Mars 2020

Andy was a Salesman at Dunder Mifflin/Sabre until being promoted to Regional Manager, starting with Season 8. He is currently not in a relationship. He was fired after Nellie stole his job causing Andy to react violently, but wins his job back after convincing David Wallace to buy out Dunder-Mifflin/Sabre, re-instating Andy as manager.



Ryan Howard, as the newest hire in the Scranton branch was initially extremely confused as to how the office worked and often acts as the straight man in his role as the temp. He is often mild mannered and meek in comparison to the louder personalities in the Office. Similar to Jim, Ryan has a poor outlook on Michael's management style and doesn't intend to work at Dunder Mifflin long term. Despite this, he's shown to be quite ambitious.
